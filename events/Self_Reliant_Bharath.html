Self-Reliant Bharath

Chennai Institute of Technology Innovation Labs hosted an impactful collaborative session with Self-Reliant Bharat on November 28. The event showcased enlightening talks by industry experts Shyam Sekar S, Venkatasubramanian N V, and Subramanian, complemented by valuable insights from Coach Murali and Dr. N Ravichandran. Centered on addressing startup challenges, the session delved into strategies for overcoming obstacles, effective business modeling, fundraising tactics, and available support schemes. This entrepreneurial gathering exemplified a commitment to fostering growth and facilitating knowledge exchange within the startup ecosystem.
